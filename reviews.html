Tom Holland

Actor

Tom Holland is a British actor, best known for portraying Spider-Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Born on June 1, 1996, in London, he began his career in the stage musical Billy Elliot before gaining widespread fame. Holland made his MCU debut as Spider-Man in Captain America: Civil War (2016) and has since starred in several Spider-Man films, including Homecoming, Far From Home, and No Way Home.
